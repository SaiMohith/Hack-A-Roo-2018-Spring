Prescription Opioid Painkillers Now Kill More Americans than Guns

Photo Courtesy of: Gregor Fischer

We have used different data sets from the Big Cities Health Coalition and the Centers for Disease Control and Prevention in order to visualize and then design a model to get the areas of Opioid Overdose. Using the model, we proceeded to predict the values in the future years. According to the Associated Press and CBS News , Opioid Painkillers such as heroin and prescription painkillers, have surpassed gun-related homicides as a higher cause of death in the United States. Opioid abuse has resulted in over 50,000 American deaths in 2015. We have used this data to consider the Opioid Overdose cases in the State of New York.
